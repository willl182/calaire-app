# Technical Finding / Milestone

**Context**: CALAIRE preprocessing boundary

## The Discovery

The CALAIRE raw-data preprocessing should be removed from the management app boundary. `calaire-app` should not initially implement the cleaning, hourly aggregation, uncertainty calculation, or moving-average generation. Those steps are better implemented offline in R inside `pt_app` as a project-specific preprocessing module.

This keeps responsibilities clear:

- `calaire-app`: management portal, authentication, rounds, participants, uploads, database workflow.
- `pt_app`: project-specific statistical workflow, preprocessing, validation, QA, and analysis preparation.
- `ptcalc`: generic reusable statistical calculations only.

A copy of the plan was saved in `pt_app/plan_preprocesamiento.md` and the versioned plan lives in `pt_app/logs/plans/260424_1624_plan_preprocesamiento-calaire.md`.
